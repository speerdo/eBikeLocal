## What to Expect Under $1,500

In this price range, you should expect:
- ✅ Hydraulic disc brakes (most bikes in this range now)
- ✅ 500–750W hub motor
- ✅ 7–8 speed Shimano drivetrain
- ✅ 40–65 mile range
- ✅ Throttle (most Class 2 bikes)
- ⚠️ Torque sensors are rare but available (Velotric Discover 1, Aventon Level.2)
- ⚠️ Mid-drive motors start above $1,500 (Ride1UP Prodigy V2 is the exception at $1,595)
- ❌ Carbon frames
- ❌ Premium suspension

The biggest thing to watch: **cadence vs. torque sensor**. Most bikes in this range use cadence sensors, which make the motor feel surge-y. If you want a natural ride feel, the Velotric Discover 1 is your best option under $1,000 with a torque sensor.
